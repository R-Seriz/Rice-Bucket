About
The inception of Rice Bucket began from an unserious thought I conceived during my junior year in high school when I felt inspired by the passionate creators who were also close and dear friends of mine. The name was something K.K. and I came up with over lunch.

The basic idea was to form a close-knit group of companions who would work together as creatives to produce works across a blend of mediums. Or, something like that.

And we're still here. Welcome to Rice Bucket.

- R.S.
